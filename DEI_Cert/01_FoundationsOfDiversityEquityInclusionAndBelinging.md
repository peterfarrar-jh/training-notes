## 3. Belonging Best Practices

### Why belonging matters

Everyone able to do their own dance.  

#### Assesment

Engagement surveys can help identify if people feel connected, and identify root causes.  

Regularly hold conversations about inclusion.  
Proactivly identify bias.  

#### Stances on equity

"Would you tolerate this prejudice for more established discrimination practices, such as harassment?""  

These measures shape how your employees feel, show up, and ultimately stay in the workforce.  

### Interrupting migroaggressions

**Microaggressions** are subtle verbal and nonverbal slights towards people due to their identity group.  

#### Was it intended?

Would you say it to the majority group?  

How not to respond to feedback:  
"Oh, I think you misunderstood me."  
"You're blowing this out of proportion."  

Practice cultural humility.
